Ultimate Platform Capabilities

IIAP OM is a high-performance ecosystem designed to handle everything from project logic to hardware logistics.

Project Management

Task Life-Cycle

Complete task orchestration from creation to archival. Supports priority levels, estimated effort, and multi-user assignments.

Multi-Level Modules

Organize projects into logical modules with dedicated forums, documents, and specific team permissions.

Requirement Versioning

Formal requirement specification with approval workflows, ensuring no development starts without a clear business scope.

Inventory & Supply Chain

Serialized Tracking

Track high-value assets using individual serial numbers. Integrated with stock-in, stock-out, and rental workflows.

Advanced Procurement

Automated purchase requisition based on low-stock triggers. Integrated supplier management and price tracking.

Reconciliation & Audits

Perform periodic stock audits with automated adjustment logs. Zero-tolerance for unaccounted stock movements.

Governance & Finance

Budgetary Management

Track project spending against allocated budgets with automated variance alerts.

Deep Audit Logs

Every sensitive action is cryptographically logged to the system audit trail for security compliance.

Knowledge & Support

Project Knowledge Base

A built-in Wiki system for project documentation, onboarding guides, and shared team notes.

System-Integrated Helpdesk

Report platform issues and track their resolution directly from your dashboard.

Real-time Alerts

Instant in-app and email notifications for assignments, mentions, and due dates.

Dynamic UI

Full support for Dark Mode, Compact Sidebar, and personalized brand color preferences.

Document Engine

High-fidelity PDF generation for Project Summaries, Stock Invoices, and Specifications.
